Grindelwald

Habitaciones

Habitacion Junior

Our comfortable junior suites measure approximately 35 m² and include one bedroom, a living area and a bathroom with bath. From the own balcony you have the views of the Alpine panorama. The junior suites are located in the main building and can accommodate up to two persons, two extra beds are possible.

Eiger Suite

Our cosy Eiger Suite measures approximately 60 m² and includes two bedrooms, two bathrooms with bath/shower, a living area and own balcony with views of the Alpine panorama. The Eiger-Suite is located in the main building and can accommodate up to four persons, two extra beds are possible.

Jungfrau Suite

Our cosy Jungfrau Suite measures approximately 80 m² and includes two bedrooms, two bathrooms with bath/shower, a living area and own balcony with views of the Alpine panorama. The Jungfrau – Suite is located in the main building and can accommodate up to four persons, two extra beds are possible.
